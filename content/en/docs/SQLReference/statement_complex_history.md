# STATEMENT\_COMPLEX\_HISTORY<a name="EN-US_TOPIC_0245374770"></a>

**STATEMENT\_COMPLEX\_HISTORY**  displays load management information about a completed job executed on the primary database node. Columns in this view are the same as those in  **[Table 1](../DatabaseReference/gs_session_memory_detail.md#en-us_topic_0237122397_en-us_topic_0112535431_table75981925115018)**.
